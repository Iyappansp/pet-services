How quickly can I get a sitter?
+
Most bookings are confirmed within 30 minutes. For same-day bookings, we do our best to match you within 2 hours depending on availability in your area.
Is my payment secure?
+
Yes. All payments are processed securely via Stripe with 256-bit encryption. We never store your card details. You only pay after the service is completed.
What if my pet has a medical condition?
+
Add all medical notes, medications, and vet contacts to your pet's profile. We match you with sitters who have experience with your pet's specific needs and can administer medications safely.
