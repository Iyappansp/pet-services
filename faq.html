Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about PawCare Pro. Can't find your answer? Contact us →

Booking
How do I book a pet care visit?
+
Simply click "Book a Visit" on our homepage, choose your service, fill in your pet details and preferred time, and we'll match you with the perfect sitter within 30 minutes. You'll receive a confirmation email immediately.
Can I book recurring visits?
+
Yes! We offer recurring daily, weekly, or custom schedule bookings. Recurring bookings also qualify for a 10% discount and you'll be matched with the same dedicated sitter each time.
What is your cancellation policy?
+
Cancel up to 24 hours before your booking for a full refund. Cancellations within 24 hours incur a 50% service fee. For recurring bookings, you can pause or cancel with 48 hours' notice.
How far in advance should I book?
+
We recommend booking 24–48 hours in advance to guarantee your preferred sitter and time slot. For same-day bookings, we do our best but availability isn't guaranteed. VIP members get priority same-day access.
Our Sitters
How are sitters vetted and screened?
+
Every sitter undergoes a comprehensive multi-step process: criminal background check, in-person interview, reference verification (minimum 3 references), and completion of our pet first aid & CPR certification course. Only about 1 in 10 applicants makes it through.
Can I meet the sitter before the first booking?
+
Absolutely! We strongly encourage a free 20-minute meet & greet before the first booking so your pet and sitter can get comfortable with each other. You can request this when booking.
What if I don't like my matched sitter?
+
No problem! You can request a different sitter at any time through your dashboard or by contacting our team. We'll find you a better match, and there's no fee for switching sitters.
GPS & Tracking
How does the GPS tracking work?
+
Our sitters use the PawCare Pro app during every walk. It broadcasts their live location to you via our platform. You'll see the real-time route on a map, plus distance, duration, pace, and get photo notifications. The full walk history is saved in your dashboard.
Do I get photos during the walk?
+
Yes! Sitters send photo updates during every visit — at least 2 per 30-min walk, and 5+ per 60-min walk. Premium and VIP members also receive video clips. All photos are stored in your gallery in the dashboard.
Safety & Insurance
Are your services insured?
+
Yes. All bookings through PawCare Pro are covered by our $1 million liability insurance policy. This includes coverage for any accidents, injuries, or property damage that may occur during a booked visit or walk.
What happens in a pet emergency?
+
All sitters are trained in pet first aid and CPR. If an emergency occurs, they follow our clear emergency protocol: contact you immediately, then your emergency contact, and take the pet to the nearest vet if required. Our 24/7 emergency line is always available.
Billing & Payments
What payment methods do you accept?
+
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), Apple Pay, Google Pay, and PayPal. Payment is charged after each completed service unless you're on a monthly plan.
Are there any hidden fees?
+
Never. Our pricing is completely transparent. The price you see when booking is the price you pay. The only additions are optional add-ons (like grooming or medication administration) that you select yourself.
