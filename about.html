Our Mission

Every Pet Deserves Love When You're Away

We believe that leaving your pet behind shouldn't mean leaving your peace of mind behind. Our mission is to make premium, professional pet care accessible, transparent, and trustworthy for every pet family.

We do that by rigorously vetting every sitter, building GPS-powered transparency into every visit, and creating a platform where pet parents feel truly in control.

Safety

Our Safety Commitment

We hold our sitters — and ourselves — to the highest standards of pet care safety.

Background Checks

Every sitter undergoes a comprehensive criminal background check before their first booking.

Pet First Aid Training

All sitters complete a certified pet first aid and CPR course before joining our network.

In-Person Interviews

We interview and do a reference check on every sitter. Only 1 in 10 applicants makes it through.

Full Insurance Coverage

All bookings are protected by a $1M liability insurance policy. Your pet and home are fully covered.

GPS Every Visit

Every walk is GPS-tracked and logged. You can follow live or review the complete route afterward.

Emergency Protocol

24/7 emergency line and clear protocols for any medical situation or unexpected event.
