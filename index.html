FAQ

Common Questions

How are your sitters vetted?
+
All sitters undergo a thorough background check, in-person interview, and reference verification. They also complete our pet first aid training before their first booking.
How does GPS tracking work?
+
Our sitters use our mobile app during walks, which shares their live location with you. You can follow the route in real time, see distance, duration, and receive automatic notifications.
What if my pet has special needs?
+
No problem! You can add special instructions, medical notes, and dietary requirements to your pet's profile. We match you with sitters experienced in your pet's specific needs.
Are your services insured?
+
Yes! All bookings are covered by our comprehensive pet care insurance policy. This includes liability coverage for any incidents during a visit or walk.
